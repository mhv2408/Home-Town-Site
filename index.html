Visit Vizag

Enjoy scenic views, beautiful beaches, and rich history of this vibrant district located in Andhra Pradesh, India.

Enjoy scenic beaches

Rama Krishna beach is known for its luxury hotels, palm trees, and city culture with exotic night life.

Bucolic hill station

Bask in the beauty of Araku Valley's picturesque landscapes and waterfalls.

Explore rich history

Bojjannakonda is a Buddist site with many sculptures dating back to 4th century AD.
